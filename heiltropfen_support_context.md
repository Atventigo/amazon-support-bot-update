## PRODUCT REFERENCE

### Zeolite Clinoptilolite

Zeolite clinoptilolite is a natural volcanic aluminosilicate mineral. Our Si:Al ratio is about 7:1 (very high), meaning the structure is highly stable and metals cannot leach out under normal conditions.

**Activation process:** Raw zeolite is ground to 25–160µm, then heated to ~450°C (842°F) for several hours to remove crystalline water. This increases surface area and detox capacity. It is then micronized to final particle size.

**Why we don't use "purified" (heavy-metal-removed) zeolite:** Removing metals de-balances the structure — it would then draw iron and other metals from the body, which is harmful. Our zeolite retains its natural balance.

**Heavy metals:** All natural minerals contain trace heavy metals. Activated zeolite can only release them above 400°C — impossible during normal use. Zeolite particles are negatively charged nano-cages that attract and trap positively charged heavy metals.

**Quality:** Packed under HACCP and ISO 22000 standards. Third-party tested. Source: Slovakia.

**Product variants:**
- Standard (1x activated): particle size <20µm
- Premium (3x TMA activated): particle size <2µm — smaller size = more surface area = stronger effect. Customer feedback confirms better results.
- Capsules: 400mg per capsule
- Zeolite toothpaste: 75ml

**Dosage (powder):** 1 heaped 5ml teaspoon, 1–2x daily, 1h before or after meals. Take with a full glass of water. Avoid metal spoon. Start with once daily, can increase. After 2 months, reduce to once daily maintenance.
**Dosage (capsules):** 4 capsules once daily with 300ml water, 1h before or after meals. Each capsule = 400mg. 8 capsules ≈ 1 teaspoon of powder.

**Excretion:** Zeolite is completely removed via the digestive tract (stool). It does not enter the bloodstream or tissues.

**EU regulatory note:** In Europe zeolite is not categorised as a food supplement. In the US it can be used as a dietary supplement.

### DMSO (Dimethyl Sulfoxide)

DMSO is a natural substance derived from wood pulp (by-product of paper production). Manufactured in Slovenia (EU). Glass from Italy (EU). Sold as a solvent — pharma grade ingredients.

**Crystallization is normal:** Pure 99.9% DMSO freezes at 18.5°C (65.3°F). If solid, place bottle in hot (not boiling) water bath for 30–60 min. Does not affect quality or purity.

**Topical usage:**
1. Clean skin thoroughly — no chemicals, lotions, or residue on application area
2. Apply with included dropper or clean cotton
3. Most users dilute with distilled water; undiluted 99.9% is also common

**Q:** Does zeolite contain aluminium/heavy metals? Is it safe to ingest?
**A:** Yes, zeolite is an aluminosilicate mineral — it naturally contains aluminium and trace heavy metals as found in the earth's crust. However, Al and Si are chemically bonded in a stable structure and do not release under normal conditions. Our zeolite is 100% natural, HACCP and ISO 22000 certified, and third-party tested. For personal health decisions, please consult your doctor or pharmacist.

**Q:** Does zeolite contain graphene?
**A:** No. What you may see in magnet experiments is the natural iron content — zeolite is a volcanic mineral and naturally contains iron, which is magnetic. There is no graphene in our zeolite.
